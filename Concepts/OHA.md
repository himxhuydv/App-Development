# OHA (Open Handset Aliance)
Group of technology and companies came together to build open standard for mobile Device.
**founded by google in 2007**
first product was android an open source mobile operating system.
**Members of OHA**
include mobile makers(Samsungs,HTC,LG) and chipset maker.
**What's the need for creation of OHA**
1.Reduces fragmentation in hardware and software.
2.to make open source 
3.publicaly avaiable to everyone.
4.to iniciate technologies to the market.
**resultant**
1.Android become most famous in the world after getting published.world first leading mobile os 
2.Fast adaption of andriod by the hardware of other companies
3.led to vast ecosystem creation of apps,services and devices.
